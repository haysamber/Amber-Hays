The Artist

Amber is an artist and digital developer with a passion for multi-media projects. While she has been drawing and crafting since youth, she loves to explore the interaction between traditional design and the digital. Since college, wesites, game development, and electronic art pieces have become her neiche.

A lot of Amber's inspiration comes from her desire to conquer every media form possible. She thrives on the challenge of doing something new, and this has led to the experimenting of metal sculpture, sewing, coding, wood building, soft electronics, ceramics and more. She is always looking to push the boundaries between traditional and digital art, so if someone has a project but isn't sure how to impliment it, she's the one to help!

The Craft

The world is changing rapidly; creators of all kinds are panicking about the impact of AI on their purpose. Some artists are learning how to adapt. Amber got her bachelor's degree in Media Arts and Sciences, which studies the interaction between humans, art, and technology. Because of this, while traditional arts might always stay her favorite, she knows how to keep her skills relevent and useful to companies or partners who want her ideas. Gamification, machine learning, and 3D/digital design are becoming more and more relevant in every field, and Amber has embraced these changes to support her creative endeavors. People with the desire to create will always be necessary, because while anybody could type in a prompt and make something now, true artists will still have the most unique ideas and visions. The urge to push artistic bounderies and find new ways to share stories will keep art alive.

Art and technology have never been more intertwined! This shouldn't close doors, this should open them. Amber is determined to find those doors and create new opportunities for artistic expression.
