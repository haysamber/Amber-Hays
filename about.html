The Artist

Amber is an artist and web developer with a passion for multi-media projects. While she has been drawing and crafting since youth, she loves to explore the interaction between traditional design and the digital. Since college, wesites, game development, and electronic art pieces have become her neiche.

A lot of Amber's inspiration comes from her desire to conquer every media form possible. She thrives on the challenge of doing something new, and this has led to the experimenting of metal sculpture, sewing, coding, wood building, soft electronics, ceramics and more. She is always looking to push the boundaries between traditional and digital art, so if someone has a project but isn't sure how to impliment it, she's the one to help!

Services Description
Event Services Amber works to create fun, accessible, and functional designs for events. She has experience in event planning, interactive experiences, and environmental design/decorating.
Custom Commissions Amber is willing to take on any media to satisfy either professional or personal needs. Interactive fashion or sculpture, digital or traditonal art, business logos etc.
Project Collaboration Amber is always open to working with other creators or organizations on long or short term projects.
